Solar System Model

Introduction

For my 8th-grade science class, our teacher tasked my group and me with creating a scale model of the solar system. We were required to have physical objects to represent the sun and all the main planets at roughly the correct distances from the sun.

When brainstorming what to do, I had the idea of making a model that could rotate the planets around the sun. Maybe it could have the speed be slow down for planets that are further from the sun.
